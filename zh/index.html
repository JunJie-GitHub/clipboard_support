Privacy First

Clipboard Safe prioritizes privacy from the very beginning. We understand the sensitivity of clipboard content and have taken multiple measures to ensure your data security:

  • Local Storage: All data is saved only on your Mac and never leaves your device
  • No Internet Required: App runs completely offline, never connects to any servers
  • Zero Data Collection: Never collect or upload any clipboard content
  • Local Encryption: Data is encrypted locally for security
