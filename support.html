Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì How do I start using Clipboard Safe?

After launching the app for the first time, the system will prompt you to grant Accessibility permissions. This is necessary to monitor the clipboard. Please go to "System Preferences" > "Security & Privacy" > "Privacy" > "Accessibility" and check Clipboard Safe.

‚ùì Why do I need Accessibility permissions?

Clipboard Safe requires Accessibility permissions to monitor your copy operations (Command+C+C or Command+C) to automatically save clipboard content. This is a necessary permission for the app's core functionality. Rest assured, we do not collect or upload any data; all content is saved only locally on your Mac.

‚ùì How do I switch save modes?

In the app settings, you can choose:

  • Command+C automatic save: Every copy is automatically saved to history
  • Command+C+C manual save: After pressing Command + C to copy, press C one more time to save

‚ùì How do I search history?

Click the app icon to open the history panel, then enter keywords in the search box for quick lookup. Supports full-text search to help you quickly find what you need.

‚ùì How long is history saved?

The history limit can be adjusted in settings, ranging from 20 to 2000 entries. When the limit is reached, the oldest records are automatically deleted. You can also manually clear all history.

‚ùì Is my data safe?

Absolutely safe! Clipboard Safe:

  • All data is saved only locally on your Mac
  • No internet connection required, runs completely offline
  • Does not collect or upload any user data
  • Data is encrypted and stored locally

‚ùì How do I uninstall the app?

Drag the app from the "Applications" folder to the Trash. All local data will be deleted with the app.

System Requirements

  • Operating System: macOS 14.0 or higher
  • Architecture: Supports Intel and Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3) chips
  • Permissions: Accessibility permissions
  • Disk Space: Minimum 10 MB

Troubleshooting

üîß App won't start

Solution:

  1. Ensure your macOS version meets system requirements (14.0 or higher)
  2. Try restarting your Mac
  3. Download and reinstall the app

üîß Cannot save clipboard content

Solution:

  1. Check if Accessibility permissions have been granted
  2. Go to "System Preferences" > "Security & Privacy" > "Privacy" > "Accessibility"
  3. Ensure Clipboard Safe is checked and enabled
  4. If already checked, try unchecking and rechecking

üîß Shortcuts not working

Solution:

  1. Confirm Accessibility permissions have been granted correctly
  2. Check if other apps are using the same shortcuts
  3. Try reconfiguring shortcuts in app settings
